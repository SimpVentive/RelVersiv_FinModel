How the Model Works

Built from RelVersiv_Model_v3.xlsx. Sliders let you explore upside and downside scenarios.


1. Revenue Engine — 7 streams driven by entity ramps. Y5 slider sets target; model applies Excel growth curve proportionally.


2. Cost Structure — COGS (30%), Acquisition (20%), staff salaries (7 roles), G&A, Clinical Trials, POD Opex, depreciation.


3. Valuation — DCF plus Revenue Multiple exit. Blended EV averages both. Post-money = Exit Value ÷ VC Return.

Revenue Stream Formulas
1. Hospital Therapy
Hosp_entities × Episodes/entity × Sessions × Price
2. Physio Therapy
Physio_entities × Episodes/entity × Sessions × Price
3. Corporate Prognosis
New_orgs × Emp × Pen × Price + Prior_orgs × 20% renewal + Prior_orgs × 12% newhires
4. Hospital POD Sessions
Hosp × Episodes × 20% conv × Sessions × ₹250
5. Corporate POD Sessions
Corp × Emp × Pen × 20% POD_conv × Sessions × ₹250
6. Home Rental
Hosp × Episodes × 30% × 2 months × ₹5,000
7. POD Device Rental (PaaS)
(Hosp + Physio) × 60% adoption × ₹20,000/mo × 12
G&A and Clinical Trials
G&A — Office rental, utilities, admin, insurance. Base = ₹24L Y1 (₹18L rent + ₹6L other), ~5%/yr growth. Each year editable independently.

CT (Clinical Trials) — CDSCO regulatory provision. ₹100L Y1 (setup), ₹50L Y2 (data). Y3–Y5 = ₹0 default. All years editable.

Both reduce EBIT (not EBITDA) — they appear below the EBITDA line.
Valuation Methodology
DCF Terminal Value
TV = Y5_FCF × (1+g) ÷ (WACC−g) ÷ (1+WACC)^5
Revenue Multiple Exit
PV Exit = Y5_Rev × 10x ÷ (1+WACC)^5
Blended EV = (DCF EV + PV Exit) ÷ 2

Post-Money
Post-Money = (Y5 Rev × Exit Multiple) ÷ VC Return
