How the Model Works

Built from RelVersiv_Model_v3.xlsx. v7.0 allows fully independent per-year assumptions — click Y1 through Y5 to set each year's values separately.


Year Selector — The highlighted year button controls which year the sliders edit. Each year stores its own values for entities, pricing, and operations.


Cost Structure — COGS, Acquisition, staff salaries, G&A, Clinical Trials, POD Opex, and depreciation. Salaries and G&A/CT are also per-year editable.


Valuation — DCF plus Revenue Multiple exit. Uses all 5 years of FCF as set.

Revenue Stream Formulas
1. Hospital Therapy
Hosp × Episodes[yr] × Sessions × Price
2. Physio Therapy
Physio × Episodes[yr] × Sessions × Price
3. Corporate Prognosis
New_orgs × Emp × Pen × Price + Prior_orgs × (20% renewal + 12% newhires)
4 & 5. POD Sessions
Entities × Episodes × POD_conv × Sessions × ₹/session
6. Home Rental
Hosp × Episodes × Adoption% × Duration × Monthly_price
7. POD Device Rental (PaaS)
(Hosp + Physio) × Adoption% × Monthly_rental × 12
